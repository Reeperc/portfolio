Solidarity Project

Solidarity Project

Project Description

  • Organizing an inclusive sports event over a period of 6 months.
  • Collaborating with a team of 5 students and the DUC Dieppe club.
  • The event allowed students from Camille Claudel Middle School to discover wheelchair rugby.
  • Participation in workshops and wheelchair rugby matches.

Event Details

  • Planning and coordinating the event with the DUC Dieppe club.
  • Communication and logistical organization with participants and supervisors.
  • Creating and managing interactive workshops to raise awareness and introduce students to wheelchair rugby.
  • Supervision and refereeing of matches to ensure a positive and safe experience.

Results and Impact

  • Active and enthusiastic participation from students and supervisors.
  • Increased awareness of inclusion and adaptive sports.
  • Enhanced organizational and leadership skills among participants.
  • Established lasting connections between students and local sports clubs.

Duration

October 2022 – April 2023
